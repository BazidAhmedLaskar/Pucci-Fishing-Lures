Our Story: By Anglers, For Anglers

Pucci Fishing Lures was born on the water, not in a boardroom.

Founded on Passion

Since starting in 2022, Pucci Fishing Lures has been driven by a single, unwavering passion: to create the best possible fishing gear and make it accessible to every angler in America. We were tired of choosing between overpriced, big-name brands and cheap, unreliable tackle. We knew there had to be a better way.

We set out to engineer lures that we, as avid fishermen, would want in our own tackle boxes. That meant tour-grade performance, exceptional durability, and a price that leaves you with enough money for gas to get to the lake.

Our Design Philosophy: The Pucci Promise

Pro-Angler Input

Every design starts with input from experienced tournament anglers to solve real-world fishing challenges.

Relentless Testing

We field-test every prototype extensively in diverse US waters to guarantee it performs flawlessly under pressure.

Affordable Quality

We use premium components and smart manufacturing to deliver exceptional quality without the excessive price tag.
