The Pucci Arsenal: Our Lure Categories

Understanding the different tools in our collection is key to unlocking their full potential.

Soft Plastic Lures & Swimbaits

The foundation of any versatile tackle box. Our soft plastic lures are engineered with a proprietary formula that provides the perfect balance of softness for lifelike action and durability to withstand multiple fish. They are ideal for finesse techniques when fish are pressured, or for rigging on a jighead to mimic a variety of forage. The Pucci bass lures collection, in particular, features exceptional creature baits and worms designed for maximum action with minimal rod movement.

Shop Soft Lures on Amazon

Hard-Bodied Lures (Crankbaits & Topwater)

This category is all about triggering an aggressive response. Our crankbaits are precision-molded and feature a true-running bill design to ensure they dive to their specified depth and maintain a consistent, fish-attracting wobble on every cast. Our topwater lures, like the renowned Striker Pro, are perfectly balanced for effortless "walk-the-dog" action, creating the surface commotion that big bass can't resist.

Shop Hard Baits on Amazon

Metal Lures (Jigs, Spoons & Spinners)

When you need to get deep, deal with current, or create maximum flash, our metal lures are the answer. Pucci jigs feature sharp, heavy-duty hooks and are designed for vertical presentations or swimming through cover. Our spoons and spinners use high-quality plating to create an irresistible flash that mimics a wounded baitfish, making them a simple yet incredibly effective choice for a wide range of species, from trout to saltwater gamefish.
